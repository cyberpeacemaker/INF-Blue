### A Critical Warning: Write-Blocking

For the **storage dump**, if the computer is **off**, you would use a **Hardware Write-Blocker** (like a Tableau or WiebeTech) to ensure you can't accidentally change a single bit on the drive. However, since you are doing a **Live Dump** (the computer is on), you are using "Software" acquisition. In this case, your primary defense is documenting that you used a "Live" method and used portable tools to minimize changes.
